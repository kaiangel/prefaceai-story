## 二、头像设计

### 设计理念

融合序话Story品牌核心：
- **故事感**（不是科技感）
- **FrameSpark™ 火花/灵感**
- **条漫/漫画元素**
- **温暖琥珀色调**
- **多元题材包容**

### 生图 Prompt（Gemini 3 Banana Pro）

**推荐版本：**
```
A minimalist logo icon design, square format 1:1, centered composition.

An open book or notebook seen from above at a slight angle. Rising from its pages are 3-4 small comic panel frames (rectangular with rounded corners) floating upward in a gentle arc, getting smaller as they rise - like story frames flying out of the book.

Warm glowing sparks and light particles weave between the floating panels, connecting them like a narrative thread.

The comic panels contain abstract colorful shapes inside (NOT detailed scenes) - suggesting different story moods: warm orange, cool teal, soft purple - representing genre diversity.

Style: Clean modern illustration with soft gradients and warmth. The comic panels have subtle white borders like actual manga/webtoon frames. Slightly whimsical, inviting.

Color scheme:
- Deep charcoal background (#121212)
- Book/notebook in cream white with amber glow on edges
- Comic panel frames with thin white borders
- Panel interiors: abstract warm orange, teal, purple tones
- Sparks and connecting particles in amber-orange (#FF9500 to #FFD93D)
- Soft ambient glow around the whole composition

Mood: Stories coming to life from a book, each panel a new world - warm, creative, like magic happening on paper.

NO text, NO letters, NO readable content inside panels. Pure visual icon.
Simple enough to work as a small 200px profile picture.
```

**备选版本（强调条漫翻页）：**
```
A minimalist logo icon design, square format 1:1, centered composition.

Three vertical comic panels arranged in a slight fan/spread formation, as if being flipped through like a webtoon or manga. The panels overlap slightly, creating depth.

Warm amber sparks and light particles burst outward from behind the panels, suggesting the magic of storytelling.

Each panel contains abstract gradient colors (NOT detailed imagery):
- First panel: warm sunset orange
- Second panel: soft teal blue
- Third panel: gentle purple

The panels have rounded corners and thin white borders, clearly reading as comic/manga frames.

Style: Modern minimalist with warmth and depth. Clean but not cold. The spark effect adds energy and magic.

Color scheme:
- Deep charcoal background (#121212)
- Panel borders in soft white/cream
- Panel fills in warm orange, teal, purple gradients
- Sparks in amber-gold (#FF9500, #FFD93D)
- Soft glow emanating from behind panels

Mood: The excitement of flipping through stories, each frame a new possibility - dynamic yet warm.

NO text, NO letters. Abstract panels only.
Iconic, works at small sizes.
```

## 五、通用发布模板

### 标题公式

```
1. "核心台词"——情感点
2. 悬念开头，直到...
3. 原来/终于/才发现 + 情感升华
```

### 描述结构

```
[悬念/背景] 1-2句
[转折/发现] 1-2句
[情感升华] 1句
[主题金句] 1句
```

### Hashtag 分类

| 类型 | 用途 | 数量 |
|------|------|------|
| 情感核心 | 精准触达 | 3-4个 |
| 流量向 | 增加曝光 | 2-3个 |
| 内容向 | 增加辨识度 | 2-3个 |
| 风格向 | 吸引特定人群 | 2-3个 |
