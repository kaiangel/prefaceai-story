## 一、古典管弦 Classical Orchestral

### 古典管弦 示例 1: 命运转折
**Prompt**: Cinematic orchestral, late-Romantic style. Full string orchestra opens with a somber, sustained D minor chord. Solo oboe introduces a fragile melody — hesitant, questioning. At the midpoint, French horns enter with a noble countermelody as the harmony shifts to D major. Timpani provide gravity underneath. The piece builds through layered brass and swelling strings to a triumphant climax, then retreats to the solo oboe again — same melody, but now in major, transformed by everything that came between.

**适用故事类型**: 人生转折、从困境到希望、命运的转弯

**预期效果**: 3 分钟左右的管弦乐，从忧郁到辉煌的完整情绪弧线，适合故事高潮段

---

### 古典管弦 示例 2: 雪中独行
**Prompt**: Neoclassical, minimalist orchestral. Solo piano plays a sparse, repeating three-note motif in E minor — like footprints in fresh snow, each step deliberate and solitary. Strings enter as a distant, frozen landscape — sustained pianissimo, barely breathing. Celesta adds occasional crystalline touches like snowflakes catching light. The arrangement never exceeds mezzo-forte. The beauty is in the negative space — what isn't played matters more than what is. Slow, around 56 BPM, the pace of walking through deep snow.

**适用故事类型**: 孤独旅程、冬季场景、沉思独白

**预期效果**: 极简钢琴+弦乐的冬日氛围，安静但不空洞，适合内心独白段

---

### 古典管弦 示例 3: 晨光中的城市
**Prompt**: Contemporary classical, impressionist influence. Harp arpeggios ripple upward like sunlight climbing building facades. Flute and clarinet trade a gentle, major-key melody — unhurried, like the city stretching awake. String quartet provides a warm harmonic bed, shifting between Lydian and major modes. Vibraphone adds sparkling accents. Around 80 BPM, moderate and breathing. The feeling of standing on a balcony at 6 AM, watching a sleeping city begin to glow, coffee steam curling in the first light.

**适用故事类型**: 新开始、城市清晨、希望与日常之美

**预期效果**: 印象派风格的温暖管弦乐，明亮但不刺眼，适合故事开场建立氛围

---

## 二、中国风民族 Chinese Traditional

### 中国风 示例 1: 水墨山水
**Prompt**: Chinese traditional, ink-wash aesthetic. Solo guzheng plays a pentatonic melody in the high register — sparse, each note placed with calligraphy-like intention, letting silence speak between phrases. Xiao (bamboo flute) responds with breathy, contemplative phrases, like mist drifting between mountains. No percussion. Subtle ambient pad provides depth like the wet paper absorbing ink. Around 50 BPM or freely rubato. The sound of a Song dynasty landscape painting — mountains emerging from fog, a tiny figure on a distant path.

**适用故事类型**: 古典美学、山水意境、东方哲思、传统文化场景

**预期效果**: 极简水墨风格的民乐，大量留白，适合中国传统美学场景

---

### 中国风 示例 2: 庙会热闹
**Prompt**: Chinese folk, festive and lively. Suona (唢呐) leads with a bright, joyful melody in G major pentatonic. Dizi (笛子) adds playful countermelodies and ornamental trills. Chinese percussion drives the rhythm — tanggu (堂鼓) provides the pulse, bo (镲) adds metallic accents, bangzi (梆子) keeps a crisp pattern. Pipa plays rapid tremolo passages. Around 126 BPM, energetic and celebratory. The bustling chaos of a temple fair — sugar painting, cotton candy, dragon dancers, children laughing, red everywhere.

**适用故事类型**: 节庆、庙会、热闹场景、中国民俗、传统节日

**预期效果**: 热闹喜庆的民乐，高能量，适合节日/庆典场景

---

### 中国风 示例 3: 现代中国风融合
**Prompt**: Modern Chinese fusion, blending traditional instruments with ambient electronic textures. Erhu plays a melancholic melody in D minor — yearning, with expressive vibrato and slides. Underneath, warm synthesizer pads create a contemporary atmospheric bed. Lo-fi electronic beats provide a gentle pulse at 78 BPM. Guzheng arpeggios are processed through light reverb and delay, creating a shimmering, modern texture. The meeting point of ancient longing and modern solitude — a young person in a concrete city, carrying centuries of homesickness in their chest.

**适用故事类型**: 当代中国人的乡愁、传统与现代的碰撞、都市游子

**预期效果**: 中西融合的现代中国风，既有传统乐器又有电子质感，适合当代故事

---

## 三、电子 Ambient

### Ambient 示例 1: 深海漂浮
**Prompt**: Deep ambient, subaquatic atmosphere. Layers of slowly evolving synthesizer pads in oceanic blue tones — dark, vast, and enveloping. Sub-bass drones pulse like deep-sea currents, barely perceptible. Granular-processed piano notes float past like bioluminescent particles — brief, scattered, beautiful. No rhythm, no melody in the traditional sense — just movement and texture. Processed field recordings of water create an immersive underwater soundscape. The feeling of sinking slowly into warm, dark water — not drowning, but dissolving.

**适用故事类型**: 潜意识、梦境、深层情感探索、失重感、冥想

**预期效果**: 深邃的水下氛围音乐，无节拍，适合梦境/潜意识场景

---

### Ambient 示例 2: 城市失眠
**Prompt**: Urban ambient, nocturnal electronics. Warm analog synthesizer pad hums like a city that never fully sleeps. Distant, detuned piano notes appear and dissolve — like thoughts at 3 AM that don't quite form into ideas. Subtle sidechain-pumped sub-bass breathes in and out. Occasional field recordings — a distant siren, rain on glass, a neighbor's muffled TV — processed and abstract. No clear tempo, but a slow pulse around 65 BPM lurks underneath. The blue glow of a phone screen in a dark room, scrolling past everyone else's curated happiness.

**适用故事类型**: 失眠、都市孤独、深夜心理活动、现代焦虑

**预期效果**: 都市夜晚的电子氛围，孤独但不绝望，适合都市深夜场景

---

### Ambient 示例 3: 记忆消融
**Prompt**: Ambient, memory-dissolution aesthetic. A simple music box melody plays in a major key — innocent, familiar — but it's being slowly consumed by tape degradation: wow and flutter, dropouts, increasing hiss. Warm reverb stretches each note into ghostly trails. A distant choir pad hums a single chord, shifting imperceptibly. The music box melody becomes more fragmented, more distant, until only the reverb tail remains. Around 55 BPM, decelerating. The sound of a memory being remembered for the last time — still beautiful, but already fading beyond recovery.

**适用故事类型**: 失去的记忆、阿尔茨海默、童年远去、不可逆转的时间

**预期效果**: 逐渐消融的音乐盒主题，从清晰到模糊，适合回忆消逝场景

---

## 四、爵士 Bossa Nova

### Bossa 示例 1: 午后咖啡
**Prompt**: Bossa nova, classic Brazilian warmth. Nylon-string guitar plays a gentle syncopated rhythm pattern — the signature bossa groove, relaxed and swaying. Soft brush drums keep time with light, jazzy patterns. Upright bass walks a smooth, melodic bassline. Alto flute carries a sweet, memorable melody above — unhurried, breathing. Optional soft Rhodes piano adds harmonic color. Around 128 BPM but feels slower due to the lazy swing. A sun-dappled afternoon at a sidewalk café, iced coffee perspiring, no agenda, nowhere to be, a good book half-open.

**适用故事类型**: 悠闲午后、咖啡厅、约会、日常小确幸

**预期效果**: 经典波萨诺瓦的温暖律动，轻松愉悦，适合日常美好时刻

---

### Bossa 示例 2: 雨中散步
**Prompt**: Bossa nova meets cool jazz, rainy-day mood. Nylon guitar plays a minor-key bossa pattern — still swaying, but with a gentle melancholy. Muted trumpet plays a searching, lyrical melody with subtle vibrato — like someone humming while walking in the rain. Brush drums are soft, with occasional rim clicks like raindrops. Upright bass is warm and deep. Light rain texture woven into the mix. Around 116 BPM, leisurely walking pace. The particular joy of choosing to walk in the rain instead of taking shelter — getting wet on purpose, smiling for no reason.

**适用故事类型**: 雨天散步、微妙的忧愁与快乐混合、都市浪漫

**预期效果**: 带有忧郁色彩的雨天波萨诺瓦，浪漫而不沉重

---

### Bossa 示例 3: 深夜酒吧
**Prompt**: Bossa-jazz fusion, late-night intimacy. Solo nylon guitar opens with a complex, jazz-influenced bossa chord progression — extended ninths and thirteenths. Tenor saxophone enters with a smoky, breathy tone, improvising around a sultry melody. Upright bass provides a deep, resonant foundation. Brush drums are barely there — just whispers of rhythm. Occasional piano chord stabs add harmonic sophistication. Around 108 BPM, late-night slow. Dim amber lighting, a nearly empty bar at 1 AM, the bartender polishing glasses, two strangers deciding whether to start a conversation.

**适用故事类型**: 深夜酒吧、都市邂逅、成熟浪漫、暧昧

**预期效果**: 深夜爵士酒吧氛围，性感成熟，适合都市夜晚社交场景

## 五、流行 Indie Pop

### Indie Pop 示例 1: 青春公路
**Prompt**: Indie pop, bright and driving. Jangly electric guitar (clean tone, chorus effect) plays an infectious rhythmic pattern. Upbeat drums with crisp snare on 2 and 4, energetic hi-hat pattern. Synth pad adds warmth and width in the background. Bass guitar provides a melodic, bouncy foundation. Glockenspiel accents add sparkle. Around 118 BPM, the tempo of excited walking. Windows down on a road trip, singing along to a song you just heard for the first time, the future is a wide-open highway and you have a full tank.

**适用故事类型**: 青春出发、公路旅行、新冒险开始、年轻的乐观

**预期效果**: 明亮活力的独立流行，适合青春/出发/希望场景

---

### Indie Pop 示例 2: 毕业前的夏天
**Prompt**: Indie pop, nostalgic and bittersweet. Acoustic guitar strumming a simple major-key pattern, slightly detuned for warmth. Soft electric guitar adds shimmering arpeggios with delay. Gentle drums, emphasizing toms over snare, creating a dreamy pulse. Warm bass guitar, melodic and supportive. Subtle synthesizer pad floats above everything like a haze. Tambourine adds gentle rhythm. Around 102 BPM, the tempo of lingering. The last golden afternoon before everything changes — you know it's ending but you're still pretending summer will last forever.

**适用故事类型**: 毕业、青春告别、最后的夏天、不舍

**预期效果**: 苦甜参半的indie pop，温暖中带有告别感，适合青春尾声

---

### Indie Pop 示例 3: 便利店的光
**Prompt**: Indie pop, lo-fi warmth with urban sweetness. Soft synth chords with gentle attack, like breathing. Acoustic guitar fingerpicking a simple, comforting pattern. Electronic drums — programmed but humanized, soft kick and snappy snare. Warm electric bass, round and present. A tiny music-box-like melody from a glockenspiel. Light vinyl texture for warmth. Around 96 BPM, late-night winding down. The fluorescent glow of a convenience store at midnight — the only warm light on a cold street, buying hot milk tea, the small victory of still being awake.

**适用故事类型**: 深夜便利店、都市独处的温暖时刻、微小的幸福

**预期效果**: 温暖的lo-fi indie pop，深夜都市中的微光感，适合日常治愈场景

## 六、Lo-fi Chill

### Lo-fi 示例 1: 周日下午
**Prompt**: Lo-fi chill, warm and lazy. Rhodes electric piano plays gentle, jazzy chords with tape saturation and slight detuning. Drum loop — muffled kick, soft snare, lo-fi hi-hat with vinyl crackle layered on top. Warm, round bass guitar line — melodic but never busy. Occasional jazz guitar lick peeks through like a thought half-formed. Subtle rain or café ambience in the background. Around 82 BPM, Sunday-afternoon pace. Bare feet on a warm wooden floor, sunlight through half-closed blinds, a crossword puzzle you'll never finish, and that's perfectly fine.

**适用故事类型**: 慵懒周末、放松日常、安静独处

**预期效果**: 经典 lo-fi 节拍，温暖舒适，适合日常放松场景

---

### Lo-fi 示例 2: 考试周深夜
**Prompt**: Lo-fi hip-hop, study-session vibe. Mellow piano sample — jazzy, slightly melancholic, processed through gentle tape compression. Boom-bap style drum pattern with vinyl-crackling snare and dusty kick. Deep, warm sub bass hums underneath. Occasional record scratch and tape stop effects as transitions. A looped vocal sample — just a syllable, chopped and pitched, like a sigh made musical. Around 78 BPM, the rhythm of a pen tapping on a notebook. An open textbook, cold coffee, highlighter running dry, the library quiet except for keyboard clicks and distant rain.

**适用故事类型**: 学习/工作场景、考试周、安静努力的夜晚

**预期效果**: 学习向lo-fi，专注但不紧张，适合努力/学习场景

---

### Lo-fi 示例 3: 老照片
**Prompt**: Lo-fi ambient, memory texture. A simple piano melody recorded through what sounds like an old cassette recorder — warm, wobbly, imperfect. No drums — just the piano and layers of tape hiss, vinyl crackle, and room tone. The melody repeats with subtle variations, like turning pages in a photo album. Processed field recordings — distant laughter, a door closing, birds through a window — all muffled, faded, as if heard through years. Around 66 BPM, the tempo of remembering. Cardboard box of photographs, yellowed edges, faces you almost recognize, the smell of old paper and someone's perfume that no longer exists.

**适用故事类型**: 翻老照片、回忆往事、逝去的人和时光

**预期效果**: 极度怀旧的lo-fi质感，模糊温暖，适合回忆/追忆场景

## 七、摇滚 Post-Rock

### Post-Rock 示例 1: 风暴来临
**Prompt**: Post-rock, epic crescendo build. Opens with a single clean electric guitar playing a tremolo-picked pattern — delicate, reverb-soaked, almost fragile. A second guitar enters with a slow, ascending chord progression. Bass guitar provides a low, rumbling foundation. Drums begin with just a gentle ride cymbal, then add floor tom heartbeats. Over three minutes, layer upon layer is added — distorted guitar, crashing cymbals, thundering bass, until the full ensemble erupts in a wall of sound. Like watching a storm build from a single dark cloud to covering the entire sky. No vocals needed — the instruments tell the story.

**适用故事类型**: 情绪爆发前的积累、风暴/灾难、命运的不可阻挡

**预期效果**: 经典后摇从寂静到风暴的渐进结构，适合情绪大高潮

---

### Post-Rock 示例 2: 废墟中的花
**Prompt**: Post-rock, melancholic beauty. Clean electric guitar with heavy reverb and delay plays a simple, haunting melody in A minor — repeating, meditative. Bowed cello provides a deep, mournful voice underneath. Second guitar adds shimmering harmonics and volume-swelled chords. Drums are restrained — mostly toms and cymbal washes, no aggressive fills. Bass guitar hums a low, steady drone. The piece breathes — expanding and contracting, never truly exploding. Around 72 BPM, patient and contemplative. A single flower growing through cracked concrete in an abandoned building — beauty that shouldn't exist but does, defiantly, quietly.

**适用故事类型**: 废墟中的美、不屈的生命力、安静的坚强

**预期效果**: 忧郁但美丽的后摇，不爆发只呼吸，适合坚韧/希望场景

---

### Post-Rock 示例 3: 告别大海
**Prompt**: Post-rock, oceanic and vast. Delay-drenched guitar creates a shimmering, wave-like texture — notes rippling outward like stones thrown into water. Bass guitar plays long, deep tones that rise and fall like tides. Drums build from brushes to full kit — ride cymbal washes emulating surf. Strings (violin and cello) add an emotional melodic arc that soars above the guitar textures. The piece builds to one magnificent peak at the three-quarter mark — all instruments singing together at fortissimo — then slowly retreats, like the tide going out. Ending with just the reverb tail, ringing into silence. Standing at the edge of an ocean you're seeing for the last time.

**适用故事类型**: 海边告别、壮阔的离别、人生阶段的终结

**预期效果**: 海洋般壮阔的后摇，一次宏大的高潮后退潮归静，适合壮美离别

## 八、影视配乐 Cinematic / Film Score

### 影视配乐 示例 1: 英雄出征
**Prompt**: Epic cinematic, heroic orchestral. Brass fanfare opens with a powerful, ascending major-key theme — triumphant French horns and trumpets. Full string orchestra drives forward with energetic ostinato patterns. Timpani and bass drum provide thunderous weight on the downbeats. Snare drum roll builds tension between phrases. Choir enters at the climax with wordless "ah" — elevating the theme to transcendent heights. Around 108 BPM, marching tempo. The moment before the charge — fear transformed into purpose, individual doubt dissolved into collective courage, the banner catching wind.

**适用故事类型**: 英雄时刻、出征/决战、克服恐惧、集体力量

**预期效果**: 好莱坞式英雄配乐，铜管+合唱的史诗感，适合高燃场景

---

### 影视配乐 示例 2: 时间流逝
**Prompt**: Cinematic, time-lapse underscore. Solo piano plays a simple, clock-like ostinato pattern — steady eighth notes that never waver, like the ticking of years. Over this metronome, strings slowly introduce a wistful melody that ages and transforms. The arrangement gradually shifts — seasons passing through orchestral color: bright woodwinds for spring, warm brass for summer, muted strings for autumn, sparse piano for winter. Celesta marks transitions like calendar pages turning. Around 92 BPM, metronomic. A time-lapse of a tree through decades — budding, blooming, changing color, losing leaves, weathering storms, still standing.

**适用故事类型**: 时间流逝蒙太奇、岁月变迁、成长、四季轮回

**预期效果**: 时间流逝感的配乐，钟摆般的稳定节奏承载变化，适合蒙太奇段

---

### 影视配乐 示例 3: 真相揭露
**Prompt**: Cinematic thriller, revelation score. Low strings hold a tense, dissonant cluster — barely audible, like holding your breath. Solo piano plays isolated, questioning notes — sparse, each one landing in uncomfortable silence. As the truth emerges, layers add: col legno strings tapping like a racing heartbeat, muted brass swells, timpani rumble. The music builds not to a traditional climax but to a moment of devastating clarity — a single, sustained major chord after all the dissonance, more shocking than any explosion. Then silence. Then a lone cello, playing the main theme stripped bare. Around 76 BPM, the pace of dread becoming certainty.

**适用故事类型**: 真相揭露、悬疑解答、震惊时刻、认知颠覆

**预期效果**: 悬疑到揭晓的戏剧配乐，不协和到突然清明，适合剧情反转

## 九、儿童童话 Children's Fantasy

### 童话 示例 1: 魔法森林
**Prompt**: Whimsical orchestral, fairy-tale magic. Glockenspiel and celesta play a twinkling, ascending melody in F major — like stars being lit one by one. Pizzicato strings hop playfully underneath, creating a bouncy, walking rhythm. Flute joins with a bright, dancing countermelody. Harp provides gentle, glittering arpeggios. Occasional triangle and sleigh bells add sparkle. Bassoon adds a touch of gentle comedy in the bass. Around 110 BPM, a skipping child's tempo. A forest where mushrooms glow in the dark, where puddles are secret portals, where the bravest thing is being small and curious.

**适用故事类型**: 魔法冒险、童话森林、神奇发现、儿童探险

**预期效果**: 经典童话配乐，闪闪发光的管弦乐，适合儿童故事的奇幻场景

---

### 童话 示例 2: 睡前故事
**Prompt**: Gentle lullaby, music-box warmth. A simple, singable melody played on music box — sweet, repetitive, comforting in its predictability. Soft felt piano doubles the melody an octave lower, adding warmth. Very gentle string pad — just a whisper of harmonic support. Occasional harp notes fall like gentle rain. No percussion — the rhythm comes only from the melody itself. Around 66 BPM, breathing-and-falling-asleep tempo. Gradually becoming softer and slower, like eyelids getting heavier. A stuffed bear, a nightlight shaped like a moon, the safety of a blanket tucked just right, a parent's voice fading as sleep arrives.

**适用故事类型**: 睡前时光、安全感、入睡、温柔的爱

**预期效果**: 温柔的催眠曲，从柔到更柔，适合睡前/安全感场景

---

### 童话 示例 3: 小勇士的冒险
**Prompt**: Adventure orchestral, child-hero theme. Confident but not aggressive — a brave melody for a small hero. Staccato strings create a forward-marching rhythm, like little determined footsteps. Clarinet plays the main theme — bright, earnest, endearing. French horn adds warmth and courage in supporting phrases. Light snare drum and woodblock provide a march-like pulse. Glockenspiel adds sparkle at key moments, like finding a clue or opening a mysterious door. Around 120 BPM, adventurous walking speed. A child with a flashlight exploring the backyard at dusk — absolutely convinced there's a dragon behind the garden shed.

**适用故事类型**: 儿童冒险、小英雄、克服恐惧（童话版）、探索发现

**预期效果**: 轻快的冒险主题曲，勇敢但童趣，适合儿童英雄场景

## 十、都市 R&B

Programmed drums: muffled 808 kick with long sub-bass tail, crisp finger snaps instead of snare, shimmering hi-hats with subtle swing. Electric bass — deep, round, melodic, with occasional slides. Rhodes piano adds jazzy color with ninth chords. Reverb-soaked vocal samples float in the background — just textures, not words. Around 72 BPM, 3-AM pace. The blue light of a phone screen painting shadows on the ceiling, typing and deleting the same message, the dangerous hour when you almost text the wrong person.

**适用故事类型**: 深夜心理活动、暧昧/纠结、都市情感、失眠

**预期效果**: 现代R&B的深夜氛围，慵懒性感，适合都市情感场景

Drums — programmed but humanized, with a lazy, behind-the-beat swing feel. Brushed snare and soft kick. Electric bass — funky but restrained, with thumb-plucked warmth. A horn section (muted trumpet + tenor sax) plays soft, arranged pads in the background. Subtle wah-wah guitar adds funk texture. Around 88 BPM, sunset-stroll tempo. Golden light on brick walls, someone practicing saxophone on a fire escape three floors up, the smell of takeout mixing with jasmine, a neighborhood that feels like a warm handshake.

**适用故事类型**: 街区黄昏、社区温暖、日常浪漫、城市中的小镇感

### R&B 示例 3: 雨后屋顶
**Prompt**: Alternative R&B, atmospheric and introspective. Sparse, glitchy electronic beats — deconstructed, unpredictable, with stuttering hi-hats and deep 808 sub-bass. Ambient synth pads create a vast, wet sonic space. Distant, processed guitar — clean tone with heavy reverb, playing slow, contemplative single notes. Piano enters occasionally with minimal, melancholic chord voicings. The production feels like looking at a city from far away — everything slightly out of focus, beautiful in its abstraction. Around 68 BPM, slow and floating. Standing on a wet rooftop after the rain stops, city lights blurred through leftover drops on your eyelashes, feeling small and okay with it.

**适用故事类型**: 都市孤独但不悲伤、雨后清明、自我接纳、城市冥想

## 使用指南

### 如何选择示例

1. **先确定故事情绪** → 从"适用故事类型"中匹配
2. **再确定视觉风格** → 参考 `cross_sensory.md` 的色调/光影映射
3. **选最接近的示例** → 直接使用或作为起点修改
4. **用五层模板微调** → 参考 `bgm_prompt.md` 的五层结构做定制

### 修改示例的技巧

- **换乐器不换结构**: 保持整体描述，替换 2-3 个乐器
- **换情绪不换流派**: 保持流派框架，修改情绪词和灵魂层
- **换速度改能量**: 同样的编曲，BPM 从 72 改到 120，能量完全不同
- **加中国元素**: 任何示例都可以加入 "with subtle guzheng/erhu/dizi" 融合中国风

### Mureka API 注意事项

- Prompt 总长度控制在 50-150 词之间效果最佳
- 避免过于技术化的术语堆砌，叙事性描述效果更好
- 开头 20 词最重要 — 模型对开头的权重最高
- 具体的意象 ("rain on glass") 比抽象的情绪 ("sad") 效果好 10 倍
- 乐器数量控制在 3-5 个，超过 7 个模型容易混乱
